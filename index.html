About the lab

From genomic diagnosis to therapeutic opportunity

The team has two core aims: 1) Expanding the genetic therapy toolbox by developing new therapeutic strategies to target rare genetic disorders, and 2) Developing computational tools to accelerate target identification and prioritisation of cases for genetic therapy development. The lab is using a combination of wet-lab and dry-lab-based methods to achieve its goals and aims to build a multidisciplinary team bridging clinical expertise, wet-lab research, and computational work.
