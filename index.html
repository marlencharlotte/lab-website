About the lab

From genomic diagnosis to therapeutic possibility

Interventional Precision Genomics focuses on identifying when a molecular diagnosis can become a starting point for action. Our work combines mechanistic thinking, variant interpretation, translational frameworks, and clinician-facing resources to support more systematic therapeutic decision-making in rare disease.
